# AI Vocabulary Reference

This is the authoritative list of overused AI words and phrases that the Humanize skill replaces or removes. Consult this file on every humanize pass; the list is updated as new tells are observed.

## Guiding Principle

Do not simply swap one AI word for another. Rewrite the sentence to avoid the concept being performed in an AI way. The replacements below are starting points, not mechanical substitutions.

---

## High-priority replacements (most common tells)

- *delve* / *delve into* → just say what you mean: "look at", "examine", "get into", or restructure
- *pivotal* → "key", "important", "decisive", or nothing (often the sentence works without it)
- *tapestry* (abstract use) → cut entirely and rewrite
- *underscore* (as verb) → "shows", "confirms", "makes clear", or restructure
- *testament* → "proof", "evidence", or restructure
- *landscape* (abstract use) → rewrite to be specific
- *vibrant* → specific description of what makes it lively, or cut
- *meticulous* / *meticulously* → "careful", "thorough", or cut
- *intricate* / *intricacies* → "complex", "details of", or rewrite
- *foster* / *fostering* → "build", "encourage", "support"
- *bolster* / *bolstered* → "strengthen", "support", "boost"
- *garner* → "get", "earn", "receive", "attract"
- *showcase* (as verb) → "show", "display", "demonstrate"
- *highlight* (as verb for emphasis or significance) → "show", "point to", "note"
- *crucial* → "important", "essential", or cut
- *robust* → specific description of what makes it strong
- *align with* → "match", "fit", "suit", or restructure
- *enhance* → "improve", "add to", "make better"
- *valuable insights* → cut or replace with what the insight actually is
- *enduring* (legacy or impact use) → "lasting", "ongoing", or rewrite
- *boasts* (meaning "has") → "has", "includes", "offers"
- *interplay* → "relationship", "tension", "connection between"
- *Additionally* (sentence-opener) → restructure the sentence, use "Also," or cut
- *key* (overused adjective) → "main", "central", "important", or cut
- *serves as* / *stands as* / *marks as* → replace with "is" or restructure
- *stands as a reminder* → cut or rewrite plainly
- *is a testament to* → "shows", "proves", or rewrite
- *reflects broader* → cut the phrase, say what it actually reflects
- *evolving landscape* → rewrite to say what specifically is changing
- *setting the stage for* → rewrite plainly
- *deeply rooted* → "established", "longstanding", or specific
- *encompassing* → "including", "covering", or restructure
- *cultivating* → "building", "creating", "developing"
- *symbolizing its ongoing* → cut and rewrite plainly
- *in today's* [world / society / landscape] → cut, just say the thing
- *navigate* (abstract use, e.g., "navigate challenges") → "deal with", "handle", "work through"

## Corporate and consultancy tells

- *leverage* (as verb) → "use", "apply"
- *utilize* / *utilization* → "use"
- *facilitate* → "help", "make easier", "enable"
- *streamline* → "simplify", "speed up"
- *seamless* / *seamlessly* → cut, or "smooth", "easy"
- *holistic* → "complete", "whole", or rewrite
- *paradigm* / *paradigm shift* → cut and rewrite plainly
- *synergy* / *synergistic* → cut, rewrite to say what actually combines
- *ecosystem* (non-biological) → "network", "system", or specific

---

## Inspirational and aspirational tells

- *unlock* (potential, value, etc.) → "find", "release", "use", or restructure
- *empower* → "let", "help", "give the ability to"
- *resonate* / *resonates with* → "matches", "speaks to", "fits"
- *journey* (abstract, e.g., "your wellness journey") → cut or replace with the specific thing
- *elevate* → "raise", "improve", or cut
- *thrive* → "do well", "succeed"
- *embark on* → "start", "begin"

---

## Marketing and lifestyle tells

- *curated* → "selected", "chosen", or cut
- *immerse* / *immersive* → specific description, or cut

---

## Inflation tells

- *plethora* → "many", "lots of", or specific number
- *myriad* → "many", "lots of"
- *paramount* → "most important", "central"
- *quintessential* → "classic", "typical", or cut
